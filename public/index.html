PROJECT PARISUTHAM

Project Parisutham

2015

"A step toward cleaner surroundings", was initiated with the purpose of manufacturing phenyl, used as a disinfectant. The major employees in this project were the people who needed it the most, the survivors of the Tsunami that occurred in 2004 in Tamil Nadu, in whom Enactus REC saw an entrepreneur. We truly believe that the ultimate destination, a better, cleaner and healthier world, will be reached soon enough, and that Project Parisutham will be an inspiration for the many other wonders to come.

PROJECT DHANIYAM

2015

Project Dhaniyam

2015

"The pursuit of healthier living" aims at playing the culinary skills hidden behind the rural fabrics of the state to our strengths. It deals with the production and promotion of millet based products manufactured by the women from the weaker sections of the society. We, at Enactus REC believe that Project Dhaniyam is an earnest endeavor in unlocking the golden doors leading towards a better health whilst empowering the women.
